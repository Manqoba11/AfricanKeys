About Us

Keys Of Africa is a trusted provider of custom-made aluminium doors and windows for homes and businesses across South Africa. With over 15 years of experience, we are committed to delivering quality craftsmanship, professional installation, and lasting results.


We offer a wide range of aluminium products, including sliding doors, casement windows, bi-fold doors, awning windows, gates, and more. All products are custom-made to your exact measurements.


Our team works closely with customers to understand their needs. We use high-quality materials and modern manufacturing techniques to create durable and stylish products that improve homes and businesses.


Whether you are upgrading existing doors and windows or starting a new project, Keys Of Africa is ready to provide expert advice, quality products, and professional installation services.
